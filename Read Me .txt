Controls for Game:

Player a.k.a Red Square:

W: Jump
A: Left
S: Punch 
D: Right 

Enemy a.k.a Blue Square:

Use Arrow Keys to perform same functions

Arrow Up: Jump
Arrow Left: Left
Arrow Down: Punch 
Arrow Right: Right
